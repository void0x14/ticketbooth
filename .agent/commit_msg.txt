fix(ux): Silent refresh to prevent Loading Content overlay during add

- content_grid_view.py: Add show_loading parameter to refresh_view()
- main_view.py: Pass show_loading to ContentGridView.refresh_view()
- window.py: Use show_loading=False in win.refresh action

Root cause: Every content add triggered full GridView refresh with
loading overlay, causing visual stutter. Now refreshes silently
in background without showing "Loading content..." overlay.
